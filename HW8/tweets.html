Tweets Search

The data for this website came from this site. This dataset contains Hillary Clinton and Donald Trump's tweets from January 2016 until September 2016. This time period captures their tweets from the primary's to the height of the election. The data aims to show the national response to Clinton's and Trump's online messages. This data can be used in different analyses of how social media affected the 2016 election. Social media platforms, specifically Twitter, had a significant impact on the canditates reputation during the campaign trail and until election night. The dataset contains over 6,000 entries, but only the top 50 are ever displayed.
